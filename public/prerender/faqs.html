Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about booking, accommodations, and wedding weekends.

Top Questions

When can we book our wedding?

We book weddings after you visit the property. We want you to feel completely sure it’s your place.

Which package should we choose?

The 3-day package offers 60 hours together; the 5-day package offers 108 hours and is the most popular.

How many guests can you accommodate?

Up to 80 guests for ceremony and reception. Up to 60 guests can camp on-site.

What if guests don’t want to camp?

Airbnbs are 5–15 minutes away, and RV trailers can be delivered to the property.

What if it rains?

The clear-top gazebo seats 80 for your reception, and rain often makes for dramatic photos.

Is there electricity and running water?

Yes. The property has solar power and potable water. Shower facilities are ready for the 2026 season.

Can we bring our own vendors?

Yes. Bring your own vendors or use our preferred list—your celebration, your choices.

When is wedding season?

June through September.
