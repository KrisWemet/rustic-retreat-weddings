About Rustic Retreat

Family-owned since 2006, Rustic Retreat grew from annual gatherings into a multi-day wedding venue built for real connection.

At a Glance

  • 65 private acres near Lac La Nonne, Alberta (99 km NW of Edmonton)
  • Multi-day wedding weekends with exclusive property access
  • Cabin for 4, camping for 60, ceremony/reception seating for 80
  • Clear-top gazebo, forest trails, and curated décor collection

Our Story

In 2006, our family purchased 65 acres of forest and meadow to build an off-grid homestead. What started as a single weekend gathering turned into an annual tradition with friends and family camping, sharing meals, and celebrating under the stars.

After hosting our own weddings here in 2018 and 2019, we realized couples deserved a space where the weekend could unfold naturally. Today, we share the land with couples who want their wedding to feel like home.
