What Makes It Special

This is a private retreat, not a one-day rental. You have the land to yourselves, with room to host your ceremony, reception, and weekend gatherings without rushing the celebration.

The cabin offers a quiet space for newlyweds, while the property provides multiple ceremony spots, campfire areas, and room to explore.
