THE EVENT

One couple. One weekend. August 21st to 23rd. Up to 50 guests not including the bride and groom.

Friday, August 21st - You and your guests are welcome to arrive anytime during the day. Get settled, light the firepit, and let the weekend begin. The wedding party will need to arrive ahead of the ceremony rehearsal, which will take place Friday evening around 7pm.

Saturday, August 22nd - Wedding day. We run a unique reverse timeline designed to build toward a candlelit ceremony at 9pm as the forest glows with string lights and fairy lights.

Sunday, August 23rd - No rush. Relax, recharge, and soak in the last morning together. The property is yours until 8pm. For those who want to keep the celebration going, an optional activity day can be added to the package - think giant slip and slide, inflatable obstacle course, scavenger hunts, and everything in between. The only limit is your imagination.

THE PROPERTY

Rustic Retreat is located near Lac La Nonne and the Pembina River, just 99 km northwest of Edmonton, 5 minutes from the shores of Lac La Nonne, and 15 minutes south of Barrhead. The property spans 65 acres of forests, meadows, and groomed walking trails and is fully off-grid and solar powered.

A few important things to know:

Outhouses on site with a two-stall shower house for guests
No electrical hookups - trailer guests are welcome to boondock (maximum 15 RVs)
Limited cell service
Pet friendly - pets must be leashed in the main area but are welcome off-leash on the trails and back field

This is a rustic, nature-forward experience. It is intentional, beautiful, and unlike anything you will find at a traditional venue - but we want to make sure it is the right fit for you and your guests.

ACCOMMODATIONS

Rustic Cabin - Included. A cozy one-bedroom cabin with loft, sleeps up to four in two queen beds. Fully equipped with fridge, stove, cooking essentials, Keurig coffee machine, and a propane BBQ on the deck.

Bridal Suite Cabin - Included. Brand new this year. Where the bride and her ladies get ready on the wedding day, right on the property.

Guest Camping - Included. Guests bring their own tents and trailers and camp together on the property all weekend. No hookups, boondocking only. Maximum 15 RVs.

Nearby Lakefront AirBnB - Optional add-on. Available for the full weekend if the couple would like additional accommodation or to spend the night before the wedding apart.

For guests who prefer not to camp, hotels are available in nearby Barrhead and Onoway. For those who want to stay on site but prefer something more comfortable than a tent, RVs can be rented through RVEazy.
