BOOKING & SCHEDULING
---------------------

Q: When can we book our wedding at Rustic Retreat?
A: Every booking starts with a visit to the property. We want you to walk the land, feel the space, and know for certain that this is where you want to get married. After your tour, if you're ready to make it official, we can get everything started that same day. Or if you'd like a few days to let it sink in, that's completely fine too — we'll follow your lead. No pressure, no rush.

Q: Which package would be best for me?
A: The 3-day package (Friday-Sunday) gives you 60 hours together—perfect for tighter schedules. The 5-day package (Thursday-Monday) provides 108 hours and is our most popular option. With 5 days, guests bond before your wedding, creating a community atmosphere. We're happy to help you decide during your property visit.

Q: When is wedding season?
A: June through September—when Alberta weather is at its best.


GUEST ACCOMMODATIONS
---------------------

Q: How many guests can you accommodate?
A: Up to 80 guests for ceremony and reception. We have room for 60 guests to camp on the property—you choose where to set up. Half a dozen Airbnbs sit 5-15 minutes away around the lake, or RVezy.com can deliver trailers directly to your camping area.

Q: What if some of our guests don't want to camp?
A: Perfect! Half a dozen Airbnb properties sit within 5-15 minutes around the lake. RVezy.com delivers trailers right to your camping area—all the coziness, none of the setup.

Q: Is there a minimum guest count?
A: No minimum. Whether you're planning an intimate elopement with 10 guests or a celebration with 80, the property is yours to enjoy your way.


PACKAGES & PRICING
-------------------

Q: What's included in the wedding package price?
A: Everything you need for 80 guests is already here: exclusive property access, the newlywed cabin, flexible camping for your group, picnic tables and ceremony benches, our full Wedding Décor Collection, groomed trails, outdoor games, campfire areas, and the clear-top gazebo. Consumables like batteries and candles are available at cost.


PLANNING & FLEXIBILITY
-----------------------

Q: Can we have a rehearsal dinner or other events?
A: Your time is yours. Whether that means a rehearsal dinner, morning yoga, or late-night bonfire—you set the schedule. You're renting the land, so enjoy it however fits your vision.

Q: Can we bring our own vendors?
A: Flexibility to choose. Use our preferred vendors or bring your own team. This is your celebration, your vision.


PROPERTY RULES & POLICIES
--------------------------

Q: What are the quiet hours?
A: Your wedding night? Celebrate until midnight. All other nights, quiet hours run 11pm to 8am for generators and amplified music. Your neighbors (the owls) will thank you.

Q: Can we bring our dog (or other pets)?
A: Yes! Well-behaved pets are welcome. Many couples have included their dogs in the ceremony. Just let us know in advance so we can share a few guidelines about the property.


WEATHER & LOGISTICS
--------------------

Q: What happens if it rains?
A: Alberta weather has never stopped a party at Rustic Retreat. The clear-top gazebo seats 80 for your reception, and rain often makes for the most dramatic photos. Some of our favorite wedding shots happened during surprise showers.


PROPERTY AMENITIES
-------------------

Q: Is there electricity and running water?
A: Yes! The property has solar power for your essentials and potable water on-site. Our shower facilities provide modern comfort while keeping that rustic, off-grid feel.

Q: Is there WiFi on the property?
A: We have limited WiFi at the cabin for essential needs, but honestly? Most couples tell us the digital detox was one of the best parts of their weekend. Your guests will actually talk to each other.


LOCATION & ACCESS
------------------

Q: How far is Rustic Retreat from Edmonton?
A: 99 kilometres northwest of Edmonton — about an hour's drive. Close enough to make the trip easy, far enough that it actually feels like a true escape.


EXCLUSIVITY & PRIVACY
----------------------

Q: Do you host multiple weddings per weekend?
A: Never. When you book, the property is exclusively yours. No other couples. No overlapping events.
