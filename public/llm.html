About Rustic Retreat Weddings

Rustic Retreat Weddings is a private 65-acre outdoor wedding venue located just 50 minutes north of Edmonton, Alberta, near Lac La Nonne. We specialize in multi-day wedding celebrations where couples and their guests can enjoy an immersive experience surrounded by nature.

Our philosophy is "Ceremony Fancy, Party Casual" — we provide the space and freedom for couples to create their dream celebration without the restrictions of traditional venues.

Established: 2020 | Couples Hosted: 50+ weddings
