OVERVIEW
---------
65 private acres of forest and meadow near Lac La Nonne, Alberta. Completely off-grid (solar powered). One wedding per weekend — exclusive property access. Family-owned and family-operated.


THE CABIN
----------
The newlywed cabin sits steps from the main fire. When the celebration winds down, the couple steps away without going far — quiet, cozy, and completely theirs.

What's inside:
- Off-Grid Solar Power: All the electricity you need, powered by Alberta sunshine. No power lines on the property.
- Room for Four: Sleeps 4. Perfect for a wedding night retreat, or invite parents Sunday morning for coffee and the best recap conversation of your life.
- Morning Coffee: Keurig with pods provided.
- Comfortable Furnishings: Cozy bed, seating area, rustic touches.
- Forest Views: Wake up to Alberta wilderness in every direction. No buildings. No roads. Just trees and sky.


CEREMONY SETTINGS & RECEPTION
-------------------------------
Multiple ceremony options across the 65 acres. Most couples choose their spot by walking the grounds until a particular clearing or view feels right.

Options include:
- Sun-drenched open meadow
- Quiet poplar grove with filtered light
- Intimate forest clearings
- Under the hand-crafted gazebo pavilion

Reception: When the sun dips low, most couples move the celebration to the clear-top gazebo. Under string lights and an open night sky, the space transforms. The gazebo seats up to 80 guests.

Evening tours available to see the gazebo lit up — contact to schedule.


THE WEDDING DÉCOR COLLECTION
------------------------------
Included in every booking at zero extra cost. No per-piece rental fees.

The collection includes:
- Ceremony arches (multiple styles)
- Rustic wine barrels
- Table styling pieces and centerpieces
- Vintage accents

The collection is always growing — shaped by new finds and pieces couples leave behind. During your tour, the team walks you through the collection and helps you picture how pieces can transform the space.
