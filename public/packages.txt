PACKAGES
---------

--- THE FULL 5-DAY EXPERIENCE (Most Popular / Recommended) ---
Duration: Wednesday–Sunday or Thursday–Monday (108 hours)
Price: $7,500 CAD (2027 season)

Timeline (Wednesday arrival option):
- Wednesday: Arrival day. People trickle in. Camp setup. First dinner together.
- Thursday: The "settling in" day. Morning hike, afternoon games, evening bonfire. Walls start coming down.
- Friday: Pre-wedding festivities. Rehearsal dinner. Anticipation builds.
- Saturday: YOUR DAY. Ceremony at golden hour, reception under stars.
- Sunday: The goodbye - softer, extended. "We should do this every year" becomes a real plan.

Perfect for: Couples who want families to actually bond. Anyone who's left a 6-hour wedding thinking "I barely talked to anyone." Those who understand the best celebrations unfold naturally.


--- THE CLASSIC 3-DAY WEEKEND ---
Duration: Friday through Sunday (60 hours)
Price: $6,500 CAD (2027 season)

Timeline:
- Friday: Gates open. Guests arrive throughout the day. Tents up, weekend begins.
- Friday evening: Exploration, campfires, first wave of reconnection.
- Saturday: Your wedding day, dawn to starlight.
- Sunday: Slow morning, tearful goodbyes, promises to do it again.

Perfect for: Couples with tighter schedules or budgets, or guests traveling from far away who need defined arrival/departure times.


--- THE INTIMATE 2-DAY ESCAPE ---
Duration: Weekdays only (2 days)
Price: $5,000 CAD (2027 season)

Timeline:
- Day 1: Arrive, settle in, rehearse if you wish. Evening bonfire under the stars.
- Day 2: Your wedding day - intimate, unhurried, exactly as you dreamed.

Perfect for: Intimate elopements with just your closest people.
